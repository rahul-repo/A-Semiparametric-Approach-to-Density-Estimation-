\begin{frame}{Abstract}
\begin{itemize}
    \item Proposes a semiparametric estimator that combines parametric and nonparametric density estimates.
    \item The approach adapts to the adequacy of the assumed parametric model.
    \item A mixing parameter determines the contribution of each estimator.
    \item The method provides both flexibility and efficiency.
    \item The mixing parameter also serves as a diagnostic for model fit.
\end{itemize}
\end{frame}
